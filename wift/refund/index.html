Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Our Payment Processor and Merchant of Record

WIFT uses Paddle (paddle.com) as its payment processor and Merchant of Record. This means that all purchases of WIFT subscriptions, licences, and one-time features are transactions between you and Paddle, not between you and Peter John Potts directly.

As Merchant of Record, Paddle is responsible for processing all refund requests on our behalf.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for any WIFT purchase, contact Paddle directly:

Paddle Buyer Support: paddle.com/support

Please have your order number or purchase email address ready. Paddle will handle your request in accordance with their Buyer Protection policy.

3. Our Refund Position

While Paddle handles all refund mechanics, our general position is:

Subscriptions (Pro, Facility, Studio)
We offer a refund on the most recent billing period if requested within 14 days of the charge date. We do not offer refunds for partial subscription periods.

One-time purchases
We offer a refund within 14 days of purchase if the feature does not function as described. Please contact us at peter.potts.editor@gmail.com before raising a refund request with Paddle so we can attempt to resolve the issue directly.

Public Beta purchases
WIFT is currently in private beta — invite only. Payment and subscription functionality is live but the software is under active development. By purchasing during the public beta period, you acknowledge that you may encounter bugs or incomplete features. Our standard 14-day refund policy applies to all public beta purchases.

4. Faulty or Misdescribed Software

If WIFT does not perform as described on our website or in our marketing materials, you are entitled to a full refund regardless of the time elapsed since purchase. Contact us at peter.potts.editor@gmail.com in the first instance.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The current version is always available at: peter-potts-editor.github.io/wift/refund
